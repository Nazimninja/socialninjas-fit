Questions

Everything you want to know.

Is this plan really personalized or is everyone getting the same thing? +
Every plan is generated individually from your body weight, height, age, gender, activity level, goal, and diet preference. A 55kg vegetarian woman doing 3 days/week home workouts gets a completely different plan than a 80kg non-veg man training 5 days at the gym. Different calories, different macros, different meals, different program.
What if I'm vegetarian or vegan? +
Vegetarian and vegan plans are completely different menus from non-veg — not the same plan with chicken swapped out. Veg plans use paneer, rajma, chole, moong dal, and tofu. Vegan plans use tofu, cashew yogurt, oat milk, and plant-based proteins. All sourced from Indian grocery stores.
How does the monthly update work? +
Every week you log your weight. After 4 weeks, the app analyzes your rate of gain or loss versus your goal. If you're gaining too slowly, it adds calories. If you're losing too fast, it increases protein. If everything's on track, it keeps the plan the same. The update is shown to you for review before applying.
Do I need a gym or can I do it at home? +
Both. During the assessment you tell us where you train. Gym members get a full PPL program with barbells, dumbbells, and cable machines. Home members get a bodyweight program designed to build real strength with no equipment. Both programs have video form guides for every exercise.
What is the food like? Is it practical Indian food? +
Yes — every meal is built around what you actually find in an Indian kitchen. Dal, rajma, chole, chicken curry, egg bhurji, paneer, poha, oats upma, roti, rice, curd, and fresh vegetables from the sabzi mandi. Meal prep recipes are designed for one Sunday session that feeds you all week. Weekly grocery cost is ₹1,500–2,000.
How do I install it on my phone? +
iPhone: open the app URL in Safari → tap the Share button → tap "Add to Home Screen". Android: open the URL in Chrome → tap the three dots → tap "Add to Home Screen". It works like a native app — full screen, no browser bar, works offline.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel from your profile inside the app at any time. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
